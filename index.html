Precision where it matters.

Fern & Sons Cycle Co. is a heritage workshop dedicated to restoring the bikes that move our city. For over seven decades, our family has cared for the rides of Toronto’s cyclists — from seasoned commuters to brand-new beginners.

Values

How we treat every bike and rider.

Skilled Tune-Ups, Done Properly.

We take our time with every bike that comes in. Each tune-up is a full mechanical check, careful adjustments, and a final road test. No rushed work. No shortcuts.

What's Included

  • Brake check
  • Gear alignment
  • Wheel inspection

Small fixes, no charge.

Quick seat-height adjustments and minor bolt tightenings are always free for riders.

Rooted in the Community.

We're a family shop that's grown alongside the neighbourhood. Riders come back because we know their bikes, their routes, and their stories.

Join our Sunday rides.

Every Sunday at 10 AM, we lead a casual group ride through the city. All levels welcome—bring your bike and meet your neighbours.
